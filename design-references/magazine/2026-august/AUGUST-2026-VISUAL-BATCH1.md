| Page | Main Translation Risk | Required Protection |

**Universal Batch 1 rules:**

- Leave **15–25% extra room** for body text
- Do not place critical text over busy images
- Avoid tiny captions
- CTA text must have room to expand horizontally
- Resource/contact rows readable at normal PDF view
- Keep critical information inside **safe margins** (0.25 in inside trim)
