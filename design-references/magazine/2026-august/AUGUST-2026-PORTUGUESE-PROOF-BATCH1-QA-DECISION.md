## Production Learning Captured

Future magazine pages should be designed translation-safe.

**Translation-safe design rules learned:**

- Give text boxes more room than Spanish requires.
- Avoid tiny crowded copy blocks.
- Leave extra padding around headings and CTAs.
- Keep QR/contact/footer in protected zones.
- Keep logos/business names protected.
- Use clean grid systems.
- Expect English/Portuguese/Tagalog text expansion.
- Plan for manual CTA/QR/contact polish after DeepL.
- Raw DeepL proof should be treated as a first-pass layout test, not final brand copy.
