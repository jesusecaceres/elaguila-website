## 1. LEONIX MISSION

Leonix exists to help businesses and the Latino community connect through premium advertising, clear discovery, and open contact paths.

Leonix is not just a classifieds website. Leonix is a Spanish-first, Latino-market-focused advertising, discovery, and exposure platform.

Leonix helps:
- Businesses present themselves professionally
- Families find trusted services
- Workers showcase their skills
- Sellers reach buyers
- Restaurants connect with diners
- Landlords find tenants
- Agents list properties
- Employers hire talent
- Community members share resources

**Core Philosophy:** Every page on Leonix must reward the viewer's attention and increase the advertiser's opportunity.

Leonix does not trap businesses. Leonix amplifies them.

## 2. LEONIX TRUST MODEL

Leonix trust is built through premium presentation, clear organization, open contact paths, external proof, measurable exposure, and respect for the advertiser.

### Trust Sources
- **Premium Presentation:** Professional, clean, organized layouts
- **Clear Organization:** Logical information hierarchy
- **Open Contact Paths:** Multiple ways to reach the advertiser
- **External Proof:** Links to Google reviews, Yelp, social media
- **Measurable Exposure:** Analytics and engagement tracking
- **Respect for Advertiser:** No fear of sending traffic to business channels

### Review Philosophy
Leonix is not building a native review system right now.

**Why:**
- We do not need to compete with Google/Yelp on reviews
- We can use Google/Yelp/social links as proof when supplied
- Native reviews can unfairly damage small businesses
- Our job is to make businesses look professional and easy to contact

**External Proof Sources:**
- Google reviews
- Yelp
- Facebook
- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Business website
- Menu link
- Order link
- Reservation link
- Portfolio link
- Google Maps
- WhatsApp
- Phone
- Email
- Other relevant business links

**Intentional Strategy:** Leonix is not scared to send users to the business's own channels. Helping the business win is how Leonix wins.

## 3. THE LEONIX AD STORY FRAMEWORK

Every ad/listing tells a story through these six roles:

### Cover
**Purpose:** Immediate visual hook and identity
**Fields:** Business name, primary category, hero image, logo
**Example Sections:** Hero image zone, business identity bar
**Mobile Behavior:** Prominent image, readable title, quick category chips
**SEO/Exposure Role:** Title tag, primary category, social share image

### Hook
**Purpose:** Quick value proposition and key differentiators
**Fields:** Short summary, main service/product, price range, location
**Example Sections:** Summary line, quick info bar, service mode chips
**Mobile Behavior:** Scanable chips, clear location/status, price indicators
**SEO/Exposure Role:** Meta description, structured data, search snippets

### Proof
**Purpose:** Build credibility through external validation
**Fields:** External reviews, social links, portfolio, testimonials, credentials
**Example Sections:** Trust badges, review links, social proof section
**Mobile Behavior:** Compact review stars, social icons, quick proof indicators
**SEO/Exposure Role:** Rich snippets, social media metadata, authority signals

### Details
**Purpose:** Complete information for interested viewers
**Fields:** Full description, specifications, hours, policies, features
**Example Sections:** About section, detailed specs, hours table, features list
**Mobile Behavior:** Expandable sections, organized tabs, readable paragraphs
**SEO/Exposure Role:** Content keywords, FAQ schema, detailed structured data

### Conversion
**Purpose:** Enable immediate action and contact
**Fields:** Phone, WhatsApp, email, website, order links, reservation links
**Example Sections:** Primary CTA bar, contact card, action buttons
**Mobile Behavior:** Sticky contact bar, thumb-friendly buttons, click-to-call
**SEO/Exposure Role:** Conversion tracking, contact schema, business listings

### Exposure
**Purpose:** Amplify reach through sharing and discovery
**Fields:** Share buttons, save/like, analytics tracking, engagement metrics
**Example Sections:** Engagement bar, social sharing, save buttons
**Mobile Behavior:** Easy sharing, prominent save/like, mobile-optimized sharing
**SEO/Exposure Role:** Social sharing metadata, engagement analytics, viral potential

## 4. FIELD-TO-STORY MAPPING RULE

Every application question must be mapped to this complete structure:

### Required Mapping Elements
- **Field Key:** Exact application field name
- **Field Type:** Data type (string, number, boolean, array, object)
- **Story Role:** Cover, Hook, Proof, Details, Conversion, or Exposure
- **Output Zone:** Specific UI zone where field appears
- **Compact Card Placement:** How field appears in result/preview cards
- **Full Preview/Detail Placement:** How field appears in full listings
- **Result Card Placement:** How field appears in search results
- **Results/Filter/Search Placement:** How field appears in filters/search
- **Landing Page Role:** How field appears on category landing pages
- **Dashboard/Analytics Role:** How field appears in advertiser dashboards
- **SEO/Share/Structured-Data Role:** How field appears in SEO/metadata
- **Public/Private/Admin/Payload Classification:** Data access level
- **Visual Treatment:** Typography, color, spacing, icons
- **Mobile Treatment:** Mobile-specific presentation rules
- **Reason for Placement:** Business justification for field location

### Implementation Rule
**No UI work can begin until this mapping exists for the category.**

Every field must have a documented reason for its placement and visual treatment.

## 6. CONTACT AND CONVERSION RULES

Leonix should allow advertiser contact paths when supplied:

### Primary Contact Paths
- **Phone:** Click-to-call, formatted display
- **WhatsApp:** Click-to-chat, formatted number
- **Email:** Click-to-mail, spam-protected
- **Website:** External link, new tab

### Social/External Proof Paths
- **Instagram:** Profile link, preview if available
- **Facebook:** Page link, preview if available
- **TikTok:** Profile link, video preview if available
- **YouTube:** Channel link, video preview if available
- **Google Review Link:** Direct to Google reviews
- **Yelp Review Link:** Direct to Yelp reviews

### Business-Specific Paths
- **Menu Link:** Direct to menu (restaurants)
- **Order Link:** Direct to ordering platform
- **Reservation Link:** Direct to booking system
- **Quote Link:** Direct to quote request
- **Directions/Map Link:** Direct to Google Maps
- **Portfolio Link:** Direct to portfolio/work samples

### Contact Rules
1. **Contact info belongs in CTA/contact zones** - never randomly placed
2. **Social/external links belong in social proof/contact zones** - organized and labeled
3. **Primary CTA must be easy to find** - visually prominent, accessible
4. **Mobile CTAs must be thumb-friendly** - 44px minimum, good spacing
5. **Do not hide business links out of fear of external traffic** - external traffic = success
6. **Leonix amplifies the business** - we succeed when advertisers succeed

### Visual Contact Hierarchy
1. **Primary CTA** - Most important action (call, WhatsApp, order)
2. **Secondary CTAs** - Important alternatives (website, email, menu)
3. **Social Links** - Proof and additional contact (Instagram, Facebook)
4. **External Proof** - Reviews and validation (Google, Yelp)

## 7. MOBILE-FIRST RULES

Most users will use phones or tablets. Design priority: phone-first, tablet-second, desktop-third.

### Phone-First Design
- **Viewport:** 375px minimum, responsive up to tablets
- **Touch Targets:** 44px minimum height, 8px minimum spacing
- **Typography:** Readable without zoom, strong contrast
- **Navigation:** Thumb-friendly, accessible patterns

### Tablet-Second Design
- **Viewport:** 768px+ optimization
- **Layout:** Two-column where appropriate
- **Interaction:** Touch and cursor support
- **Content:** Enhanced detail, better use of space

### Desktop-Third Design
- **Viewport:** 1024px+ enhancement
- **Layout:** Multi-column, hover states
- **Interaction:** Keyboard and mouse support
- **Content:** Maximum detail, premium presentation

### Mobile Requirements
- **No Horizontal Overflow:** Content must fit viewport
- **Readable Typography:** Strong fonts, good contrast
- **Sticky Contact Actions:** Contact available when needed
- **Fast Loading:** Optimized for mobile networks
- **Mobile Content = Desktop Content:** No weakened mobile experience

### Touch Optimization
- **44px minimum touch targets**
- **8px minimum spacing between targets**
- **Thumb-friendly zones** (bottom 50% for primary actions)
- **Visual feedback** on touch
- **Accessible gestures** (tap, swipe, pinch)

## 9. PREMIUM VISUAL SYSTEM

The Leonix look is warm, professional, and trustworthy.

### Color Palette
- **Page Background:** `#F4F1EB` (warm cream)
- **Card Surface:** `#FFFAF3` (soft ivory)
- **Elevated Chip Surface:** `#F6EBDD` (warm ivory)
- **Border:** `#D8C2A0` (champagne gold)
- **Primary Text:** `#1F1A17` (deep espresso)
- **Secondary Text:** `#5A5148` (muted cocoa)
- **Muted Text:** `#8B7E70` (soft gray)
- **Gold Accent:** `#BEA98E` (soft gold)
- **Dark CTA:** `#2C1810` (rich espresso)
- **Success Green:** `#1A4D2E` (forest green)
- **Info Blue:** `#355C7D` (ocean blue)
- **Error Red:** `#A04444` (warm red)

### Typography Rules
- **No Thin Washed-Out Text:** All text must be strong and readable
- **Bold Hierarchy:** Use `font-bold` for titles and important elements
- **Semibold Support:** Use `font-semibold` for chips and CTAs
- **Readable Sizes:** Minimum 16px for body text on mobile
- **Strong Contrast:** Ensure WCAG AA compliance

### Layout Principles
- **Generous Spacing:** 4px spacing scale, roomy layouts
- **Rounded Corners:** `rounded-2xl` and `rounded-3xl` for cards
- **Soft Shadows:** Subtle, premium shadow effects
- **Clear Hierarchy:** Visual organization through size, color, spacing
- **Consistent Patterns:** Reusable components and layouts

### Chip Design
- **No Beige-on-Beige:** Ensure readable contrast
- **Clear Background:** Use elevated chip surface
- **Strong Text:** Primary text color, semibold weight
- **Proper Borders:** Champagne gold borders
- **Adequate Padding:** Comfortable touch targets

### CTA Design
- **Dark Primary CTA:** Rich espresso background
- **High Contrast:** White or light text on dark
- **Touch-Friendly:** 44px minimum height
- **Clear Labels:** Action-oriented, concise text
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Primary > Secondary > Tertiary

### Emoji/Icon Usage
- **Strategic Markers:** Use for scanability and quick recognition
- **Cultural Relevance:** Appropriate for Latino market
- **Not Overused:** Don't make UI childish or cluttered
- **Meaningful:** Each icon should have clear purpose
